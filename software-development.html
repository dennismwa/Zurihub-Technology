What We Do

What Is Custom Software Development?

Custom software development is the process of designing, creating, deploying, and maintaining software applications tailored specifically to your business needs. Unlike off-the-shelf solutions that force you to adapt your workflow, custom software adapts to the way you work.

At Zurihub Technology, we specialize in building enterprise-grade business systems — from CRM and POS platforms to complex ERP and banking solutions. Our developers work closely with your team to understand your unique challenges, processes, and goals, then engineer solutions that fit like a glove.

Every system we build is designed for real-world use: intuitive interfaces your staff can learn quickly, robust backends that handle thousands of transactions, and flexible architectures that scale as your business grows.

Our Process

How We Build Your Custom Software

Our agile development process ensures transparency, quality, and timely delivery at every milestone.

1

Business Analysis

We study your current workflows, pain points, user roles, and operational challenges through interviews, process mapping, and requirements documentation.

2

System Architecture

Our architects design the database schema, API structure, module breakdown, user interface wireframes, and integration points for your approval.

3

Agile Development

We develop in two-week sprints, delivering working features you can test and provide feedback on. This iterative approach ensures the final product meets your exact needs.

4

Quality Assurance

Rigorous testing including unit tests, integration tests, performance testing, security audits, and user acceptance testing (UAT) with your team.

5

Deployment & Training

We deploy to your servers or cloud infrastructure, migrate existing data, configure integrations, and provide hands-on training for your entire team.

6

Ongoing Support

Continuous maintenance with bug fixes, security patches, performance monitoring, feature enhancements, and 24/7 technical support.

Getting Started

What You Need Before Starting a Software Project

Having these elements prepared helps us deliver a system that perfectly fits your operations from the start.

Business Process Documentation

A description of your current workflows, even if they are on paper or in spreadsheets. We will help formalize them into a digital system.

User Roles & Permissions

Identify who will use the system and what each person or department should be able to access, view, edit, or approve.

Existing Data

Any data you want migrated into the new system — customer lists, product catalogs, financial records, employee data — in any format.

Integration Requirements

List any third-party services you need connected — M-Pesa, bank APIs, accounting software, SMS gateways, email marketing tools.

Key Decision Maker

Assign a project champion from your side who can make decisions, provide feedback on designs, and coordinate testing with your team.

Budget & Priority Features

Define your budget range and prioritize must-have vs. nice-to-have features so we can plan phased delivery for maximum value.
