What It Is

What Is a Real Estate CRM?

A Real Estate CRM is a customer relationship management platform purpose-built for the property industry. It goes far beyond generic CRM functionality by incorporating property listing management, lead capture from multiple channels, viewing scheduling, offer tracking, transaction management, agent performance monitoring, and commission processing.

At Zurihub Technology, we build custom real estate CRM solutions that address the unique needs of the Kenyan property market. Our systems help real estate companies manage the complete property sales and rental lifecycle — from listing a property and capturing interested buyers to negotiating, closing, and managing post-sale relationships.

Whether you are a real estate agency, property developer, land broker, or rental management company, our CRM gives you the tools to convert more leads, close deals faster, track agent performance, and grow your property business.

Property Listings Management
