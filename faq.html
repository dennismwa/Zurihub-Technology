SEO & Marketing

Search Engine Optimization & Digital Marketing

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the process of optimizing your website to rank higher in search engine results like Google. Here's why your business needs it:

  • Increased Visibility: Appear on the first page of Google when people search for your products or services
  • More Traffic: Drive organic (free) visitors to your website
  • Build Credibility: Higher rankings signal trustworthiness to potential customers
  • Cost-Effective: Unlike paid ads, organic traffic doesn't cost per click
  • Long-Term Results: SEO delivers compounding returns over time
  • Competitive Advantage: Outrank your competitors in search results

Our SEO services include keyword research, on-page optimization, technical SEO, content strategy, and link building. Explore our SEO services.

SEO is a long-term investment, and results build over time:

  • Month 1-2: Technical fixes, on-page optimization, keyword research
  • Month 2-3: Initial improvements in rankings and indexing
  • Month 4-6: Noticeable increase in organic traffic and keyword positions
  • Month 6-12: Significant results with sustained ranking improvements

The exact timeline depends on your industry competition, website history, and the aggressiveness of the strategy. We provide detailed monthly reports to track your progress and ROI.

Working With Zurihub

Starting a project with Zurihub is simple and straightforward:

  1. Contact Us: Reach out via phone (+254 758 256 440), WhatsApp, email, or our quote form
  2. Free Consultation: We schedule a consultation to understand your business, goals, and requirements
  3. Proposal & Quote: We provide a detailed proposal with scope, timeline, and pricing
  4. Agreement & Deposit: Upon approval, we finalize the agreement and you provide the initial deposit
  5. Development Begins: Our team starts working with regular updates and progress reports

  7. Launch & Handover: We deploy the project and provide full documentation and training

To ensure a smooth start, we recommend having the following ready:

  • Budget & Timeline: Your budget range and preferred launch date

Don't worry if you don't have everything ready — we'll guide you through the entire process during our consultation. The most important thing is having a clear idea of what you want to achieve.
