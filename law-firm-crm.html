What It Is

What Is a Law Firm CRM?

A Law Firm CRM is a specialized customer relationship management and practice management platform built for the legal profession. It combines traditional CRM capabilities — contact management, communication tracking, and business development — with legal-specific features like case management, time billing, document management, court calendaring, and trust accounting.

At Zurihub Technology, we build custom legal practice management solutions that address the unique needs of Kenyan law firms. Our systems manage the complete client journey from intake and conflict checks through active case management, billing, and post-matter follow-up, ensuring nothing falls through the cracks.

Whether you are a solo practitioner, a boutique firm, or a large multi-practice law firm, our systems are designed to increase billable hours captured, reduce administrative overhead, improve client communication, and ensure no deadline is ever missed.
