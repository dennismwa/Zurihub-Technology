What It Is

What Is a POS System?

A Point of Sale (POS) system is the central hub where business transactions happen. It combines hardware and software to process sales, manage inventory, track payments, and generate real-time business reports — replacing manual cash registers with intelligent automation.

At Zurihub Technology, we build custom POS systems tailored to your specific business type. Whether you run a retail shop, supermarket, restaurant, or pharmacy, our POS solutions are designed to handle high-volume transactions, integrate with M-Pesa and card payments, and provide the data insights you need to grow.

Our POS systems go beyond just processing sales — they manage your entire inventory lifecycle, track employee performance, handle customer loyalty programs, and provide owner-level dashboards accessible from anywhere.

Getting Started

What You Need for a POS System

Prepare these elements to ensure a smooth POS system implementation for your business.

Product Catalog

A list of all products/services with prices, categories, SKUs, and barcodes (if available) for bulk upload into the POS system.

Payment Methods

Confirm which payment methods you need: M-Pesa (till number or paybill), card terminals, cash, bank transfer, or credit accounts.

Hardware Requirements

Identify needed hardware: computers/tablets, receipt printers, barcode scanners, cash drawers, and weighing scales. We can advise and source.

Number of Branches

Specify how many locations need POS terminals and whether you need centralized management across branches.

Staff & User Roles

Define user types: cashiers, floor managers, inventory clerks, accountants, and owners — each with different access levels.

Internet Connectivity

Reliable internet for cloud sync and M-Pesa processing. Our offline mode ensures operations continue during outages.
