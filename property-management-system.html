What It Is

What Is a Property Management System?

A Property Management System (PMS) is a comprehensive software platform that helps landlords, property managers, and real estate companies manage their property portfolios efficiently. It centralizes tenant information, automates rent collection, tracks maintenance requests, manages leases, and generates financial reports — all from one dashboard.

At Zurihub Technology, we build custom property management solutions designed for the Kenyan real estate market. Our systems feature M-Pesa rent collection, automated SMS reminders, tenant portals, caretaker apps, and landlord dashboards that give you complete control over your properties from anywhere.

From individual landlords with a handful of units to large real estate companies managing hundreds of properties, our PMS scales to meet your exact needs while reducing administrative overhead and maximizing rental income.
