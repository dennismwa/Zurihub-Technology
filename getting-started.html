Getting Started

Everything you need to know and prepare before starting a project with Zurihub Technology. We've made the process simple and straightforward.

Project Requirements

What You Need Before
Starting a Project

To ensure a smooth, efficient project delivery, we recommend having these elements prepared before we begin.

Clear Business Goals

Define what you want to achieve — increased sales, online presence, automation, or customer engagement.

Content & Images

Prepare your text content, product descriptions, team bios, and high-quality images for the project.

Branding Materials

Your logo, brand colors, fonts, and existing brand guidelines to maintain visual consistency.

Domain & Hosting

A registered domain and hosting plan. We can assist with setup and recommend the best options.

Project Deposit

An initial deposit to confirm commitment and begin the project. Payment plans available for larger projects.

Timeline Expectations

Share your preferred launch date and key deadlines so we can plan and deliver accordingly.
