Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, bank transfers, and international payments via PayPal or wire transfer. We offer flexible payment plans - typically 50% deposit to start, and 50% upon completion.

How long does a typical project take?

Basic websites: 1-2 weeks. Business websites: 2-4 weeks. E-commerce: 3-6 weeks. Custom software: 4-12 weeks depending on complexity. We provide detailed timelines during the quotation process.

What are your typical price ranges?

Simple corporate websites start from KES 20,000; complex ecommerce and custom web apps from KES 50,000 to KES 300,000+. Ecommerce from KES 30,000 (negotiable); custom integrations KES 80,000–150,000+. Basic CRM or POS from KES 60,000 and KES 25,000 respectively; enterprise ERP from KES 150,000 to KES 700,000+. WordPress from KES 17,000; Shopify setup from KES 20,000. All prices are indicative — we provide detailed quotes after understanding your needs.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes! All packages include a support period. After that, we offer affordable maintenance packages starting from KSh 5,000/month for basic support, with options for more comprehensive coverage.

Can I upgrade my package later?

Absolutely! Our solutions are built to scale. You can start with a basic package and add features or upgrade to a higher tier as your business grows. We design with future expansion in mind.
