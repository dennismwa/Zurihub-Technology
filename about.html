Our Story

From a Vision to a Full-Service Technology Company

Zurihub Technology was founded in 2022 by Dennis Mwangi as a vision to empower businesses with innovative digital solutions. What started as a small web development venture has grown into a full-service technology company delivering websites, software systems, and digital marketing solutions to 100+ businesses across Kenya and beyond.

Our journey has been driven by a passion for technology and an unwavering commitment to our clients' success. We believe that every business, regardless of size, deserves access to world-class digital tools that can transform their operations and accelerate growth.

Today, Zurihub Technology stands as a trusted partner for businesses seeking to navigate the digital landscape. From stunning websites to powerful enterprise systems like CRM, POS, and ERP solutions, we deliver end-to-end technology services that make a real difference.
