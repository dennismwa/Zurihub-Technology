What It Is

What Is a Banking & Loan Management System?

A Banking and Loan Management System is a specialized financial software platform that automates the entire lending lifecycle — from loan application and approval to disbursement, repayment tracking, and collections. It also manages member accounts, savings products, share capital, and regulatory reporting.

At Zurihub Technology, we build custom financial systems for SACCOs, microfinance institutions (MFIs), chamas, and lending companies operating in Kenya. Our platforms feature M-Pesa integration for seamless disbursement and repayment, credit scoring algorithms, automated interest calculations, and compliance with CBK and SASRA regulations.

Whether you are launching a new digital lending platform or modernizing an existing SACCO system, our solutions handle the complexity of financial operations while providing a smooth experience for both staff and members.
