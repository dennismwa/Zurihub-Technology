What Is Web Development?

Web development is the process of planning, designing, building, and maintaining websites and web applications. At Zurihub Technology, we go beyond just writing code — we create complete digital experiences that serve as the foundation of your online business presence.

Our web development services encompass everything from simple informational websites to complex, data-driven web applications. We combine cutting-edge front-end technologies with robust back-end architectures to deliver websites that are fast, secure, scalable, and optimized for search engines.

Whether you are a startup launching your first website or an established business looking to modernize your digital presence, our team of experienced developers and designers will craft a custom solution that aligns perfectly with your business objectives.

Getting Started

What You Need Before Starting a Web Project

To ensure an efficient development process and outstanding results, we recommend having these elements prepared before we begin.

Clear Business Goals

Define your primary website objectives: brand awareness, lead generation, online sales, information sharing, or customer support.

Domain Name

A registered domain name (e.g., yourbusiness.co.ke). We can assist with domain registration and recommend the best options.

Content & Images

Prepare your text content (about us, services, product descriptions) and high-quality images. We also offer content writing services.

Branding Materials

Your logo files, brand color palette, preferred fonts, and any existing brand guidelines for visual consistency.

Reference Websites

Share 2-3 websites you admire for design, functionality, or user experience. This helps us understand your taste and expectations.

Budget & Timeline

A clear budget range and preferred launch date help us plan the project scope, team allocation, and delivery milestones.
