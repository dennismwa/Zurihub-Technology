What It Is

What Is a Hotel & Restaurant Management System?

A Hotel and Restaurant Management System is an integrated software platform that manages every aspect of hospitality operations — from room reservations and front desk check-in to restaurant orders, kitchen management, housekeeping, billing, and guest communication.

At Zurihub Technology, we build custom hospitality solutions that unify your hotel and F&B operations into one seamless platform. Our systems handle online bookings, walk-in reservations, room assignments, restaurant table management, kitchen display systems, bar operations, and consolidated billing with M-Pesa integration.

Whether you run a boutique hotel, a resort, a standalone restaurant, or a chain of hospitality venues, our systems are designed to enhance guest experience, maximize occupancy, streamline operations, and increase revenue per guest.
