What It Is

What Is a Gate Management System?

A Gate Management System is a digital security platform that automates and monitors access to residential estates, office buildings, industrial parks, and gated communities. It replaces manual paper-based visitor logs with digital registration, QR-code verification, biometric scanning, and real-time monitoring dashboards.

At Zurihub Technology, we build intelligent gate management solutions that give property managers complete control over who enters and exits their premises. Our systems track visitors, service providers, delivery personnel, and vehicles with full audit trails and instant notifications to residents.

From small residential courts to large commercial complexes, our gate management systems are designed for security, convenience, and scalability — ensuring every entry is authorized, logged, and traceable.

Key Features

Comprehensive Access Control Features

Every feature designed to maximize security while maintaining smooth, convenient access for authorized personnel.

Visitor Pre-Registration

Residents register visitors in advance via mobile app or web portal. Visitors receive QR codes or PINs for quick, paperless gate entry with instant host notification.

Biometric Access Control

Fingerprint scanning, facial recognition, and RFID card readers for secure, contactless entry for residents, staff, and authorized personnel.

Vehicle Tracking

Number plate registration, parking allocation, unauthorized vehicle alerts, and optional ANPR camera integration for automated vehicle identification.

Real-Time Notifications

Instant SMS, WhatsApp, or push notifications to residents when visitors arrive, plus alerts for security events, denied access attempts, and emergencies.

Security Dashboard

Real-time monitoring dashboard showing active visitors, entry/exit logs, guard activity, security alerts, and analytics on traffic patterns.

CCTV Integration

Seamless integration with existing CCTV and IP camera systems for video verification, recording of all gate events, and live surveillance feeds.
