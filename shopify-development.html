What Is Shopify Development?

Shopify is the world's leading ecommerce platform, powering over 4 million online stores globally. Shopify development involves setting up, customizing, and optimizing your Shopify store to create a unique shopping experience that reflects your brand and maximizes conversions.

At Zurihub Technology, our Shopify experts handle everything from initial store setup and theme customization to advanced Liquid template development, app integration, and platform migration. We ensure your Shopify store is not just visually stunning but also optimized for speed, SEO, and conversion rates.

Whether you are launching your first online store or migrating from another platform, we leverage Shopify's powerful ecosystem to build a store that is easy to manage, scales with your growth, and provides the world-class shopping experience today's customers expect.

Getting Started

What You Need for a Shopify Store

Prepare these elements and we will handle the rest — from setup to launch.

Shopify Subscription

A Shopify plan (Basic, Shopify, or Advanced) starting at $39/month. We help you choose the right plan for your business size and needs.

Product Information

Product names, descriptions, prices, categories, variants, and high-quality photos for all items you want to sell.

Brand Assets

Your logo, brand colors, fonts, and any design preferences or reference stores you admire for visual direction.

Payment Credentials

M-Pesa business details, bank account information for card payments, and any preferred payment gateway credentials.

Shipping Details

Your delivery zones, shipping rates (flat rate, weight-based, or free shipping thresholds), and estimated delivery times.

Store Policies

Return policy, refund policy, and privacy policy content. We can help draft these using Shopify's built-in policy generators.
