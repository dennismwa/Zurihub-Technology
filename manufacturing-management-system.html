What It Is

What Is a Manufacturing Management System?

A Manufacturing Management System (MMS) is a specialized software platform that digitizes and optimizes the entire production lifecycle — from raw material procurement and production planning to shop floor execution, quality control, and finished goods warehousing. It gives manufacturers real-time visibility into production status, inventory levels, costs, and quality metrics.

At Zurihub Technology, we build custom manufacturing solutions designed for Kenyan and East African manufacturers. Our systems handle Bill of Materials (BOM) management, production scheduling, work order tracking, material requirement planning (MRP), quality checkpoints, and integration with ERP systems for end-to-end business management.

Whether you manufacture food products, beverages, plastics, building materials, or consumer goods, our systems are tailored to your specific production processes, compliance requirements, and growth goals.
