What It Is

What Is a Learning Management System?

A Learning Management System (LMS) is a digital platform that enables organizations to create, deliver, manage, and track educational content and training programs. It provides a centralized hub where instructors publish courses and learners access materials, complete assessments, and earn certifications.

At Zurihub Technology, we build custom LMS platforms that go beyond basic course hosting. Our solutions include interactive video lessons, live virtual classrooms, automated assessments, progress analytics, M-Pesa payment integration, and mobile-optimized interfaces designed for the Kenyan and African market.

Whether you are an educational institution moving to blended learning, a training company launching an online academy, or a corporation building employee development programs, our LMS solutions are tailored to your exact pedagogical and business requirements.

Getting Started

What You Need for an LMS Project

Prepare these elements for a successful LMS implementation.

Course Curriculum

Outline your course structure, learning objectives, module breakdown, and content types (video, text, PDF, interactive) for each program.

User Types & Roles
