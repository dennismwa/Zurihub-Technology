What It Is

What Is a CRM System?

A Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system is a centralized software platform that helps businesses manage every interaction with current and potential customers. It stores contact details, tracks communication history, monitors sales pipelines, and automates follow-ups — all in one unified dashboard.

At Zurihub Technology, we build custom CRM solutions that go far beyond generic off-the-shelf tools. Our systems are tailored to your specific sales process, customer journey, and business workflows, ensuring every feature directly contributes to your growth.

Whether you run a small business needing basic contact management or an enterprise requiring multi-department CRM with advanced analytics, we design and develop CRM platforms that transform how you attract, engage, and retain customers.

Getting Started

What You Need Before Starting a CRM Project

To ensure we build the perfect CRM for your business, prepare these key elements before development begins.

Defined Sales Process

Document your current sales stages, from lead generation to deal closure, so we can design pipelines that mirror your actual workflow.

User Roles & Team Size

Identify who will use the CRM (sales reps, managers, admins, support) and their access levels to configure the right permissions structure.

Existing Customer Data

Prepare your current customer database (Excel, CSV, or existing system) for smooth data migration into the new CRM platform.

Integration Requirements

List all tools and platforms (email, accounting, payment, messaging) that should integrate with your CRM for a connected workflow.

Custom Fields & Reports

Define what customer information you need to track and what reports or KPIs are most important for your sales and management teams.

Budget & Timeline

Having a clear budget range and desired go-live date helps us plan development phases and allocate resources efficiently.
